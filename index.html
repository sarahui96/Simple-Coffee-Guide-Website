Coffee Beans, Roasters and Identity

The two types of beans are Arabica and Robusta. Beans are mainly categorized based on the intensity with which it's roasted: Light roast, Medium roast, Medium-Dark roast and Dark roast.

Different Coffee types

Different types of coffees are distinguished on the basis of different proportions of espresso, steamed milk, milk foam and water mixed together. Most common types are Cappucino, Latte, Macchiato and Americano.
